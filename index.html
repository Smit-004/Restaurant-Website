Our Story

Grandeur Born
in the Mughal Kitchens

Royal Spice Mahal was founded by Masterchef Arjun Kapoor, whose family has kept the flame of authentic vegetarian Mughlai and North Indian cuisine burning since the 1800s.

Every recipe is a heirloom — passed from grandmother to grandson, refined over generations, never compromised. Our chefs source rare spices directly from Rajasthan, Kerala, and Kashmir, blending them the way they were intended: slowly, intentionally, and with devotion.
